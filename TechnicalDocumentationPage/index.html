Intro

This is an introduction to all the people who keep on thinking "What should I cook today?" Know that we eat not to fill our stomachs but to get the necessary nutritions for our body. The amount of nutrition we get from the food does not depend on how expensive or rare the food is. Sometimes the nutritions our body require is within the simplest and the most inexpensive food. So when you want to cook, always keep nutrition as a number one element to think about.

We have prepared this basic documentation to make your life easier. So let's get to it.

Breakfast

It is the most important meal of the day, for you get the necessary nutritions to keep you going throughout the day. When considering breakfast, you should think of light food. It is gonna be the first thing you will put in your stomach when you open your eyes. You don't want something gaseous that will give you trouble during your day. You don't something fatty or acidic as well.

Here are some breakfat items that we have listed for you. They are simple, inexpensive, and most nutritious:

  • eggs
  • dairy and dairy products
  • vegetables and fruits

These are simple, found everywhere and inexpensive items for your breakfast table. Breakfast is the most important meal for your day. Let's look at it from a computing approach.

Dinner

In the days that you do have lunch you may skip dinner or replace it with some light snacks. In the days that you do not have lunch, all that is stated for lunch is applicable for dinner. Make sure you have your dinner in the afternoon and not evening or night.

Try to keep your dinner light as heavy food may disturb your sleep behaviour.
